## About me

I'm an Engineering Manager passionate about building **high-performance teams** where people can do their best work. I believe great software is first a people problem, then a technical one.

My focus areas:

- 🧭 **Servant Leadership** — removing blockers, creating the right environment, and enabling teams to thrive
- 🤝 **Psychological Safety** — healthy team culture as the foundation of everything else
- 🚀 **Continuous Delivery** — fast, safe, and sustainable flow from idea to production
- 📣 **Sharing & Learning** — I write and speak about engineering culture, delivery, and leadership
